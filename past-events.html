Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, ICTE, was held on April 2 - 4, 2017, in Barcelona, Spain. ICTE is one of the 5 conferences that make up the World Congress on Civil, Structural, and Environmental Engineering (CSEE). Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the congress becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Hayder H. Abdullah
    for the paper entitled: "Stabilisation of Clay with Fly-Ash Geopolymer Incorporating GGBFS"
  • Dr. Ahmed Fahmy
    for the paper entitled: "Uplift and Lateral Performance of Tapered Helical Piles in Sand"
  • Dr. Harry Far
    for the paper entitled: "Long-Term Structural Behaviour of Composite Sandwich Panels"
  • Dr. Ikuo Tanabe
    for the paper entitled: "Consideration Regarding Environmentally-Friendly in Manufacturing Field"
  • Jens Maggen
    for the paper entitled: "Removal of Heavy Metals in Water with Adsorbents Derived from Pig Manure"
  • Dr. Samy Mahmoud
    for the paper entitled: "Efficient Real-Time Allocation of Patrol Cars in Traffic Management"
