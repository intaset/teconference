Transporation methods

  • A Taxi costs about 35 euros and takes 20-25 minutes. The taxi service in Barcelona are yellow and black cars. All taxis have licence number shown on the door and they need to use the taximeter at all time to calculate the fare rate. Rates are show on all taxis windows.
  • The AEROBUS is a bus that departs from T1 or T2 and arrives at Catalunya Square, it takes about 20 minutes. Then take the green line metro to Mundet which takes about 10-15 minutes. The hotel is on the same street as the metro's stop. It costs 6 euros for the AEROBUS and 2.10 euros for the metro.
  • Take the T2 train from the airport to Catalunya square or Sants station. From there, take the green line to Mundet. It costs 4 euros for the train and 2 euros for the metro.
